About Weather App

Our Weather App gives you real-time weather updates with beautiful themes. It changes background images based on weather (sunny, cloudy, rainy, snow, etc.) so you always feel the live atmosphere while checking the forecast.

Who We Are

We are a passionate team of developers and designers who love creating smart, user-friendly, and visually attractive apps. Our goal is to make checking the weather both useful and fun.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, simple, and beautiful weather insights that help people plan their day better. We believe weather apps should not only inform but also inspire.
